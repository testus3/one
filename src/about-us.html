About Scryper

Scryper is a software company that specializes in creating mobile and computer applications powered by AI. Our team utilizes Figma, React, Node, Express, Django, and Spring Boot for frontend and backend development, ensuring innovative and responsive user experiences. In the backend, we rely on Spring Boot, REST, Django, MongoDB, MySQL, and PostgreSQL for data management, AI integration, and API development.

Our mobile apps, crafted with Flutter, seamlessly incorporate Machine Learning and AI capabilities using Firebase for real-time features. We streamline deployment with Docker and Kubernetes while harnessing AWS for robust cloud infrastructure. At Scryper, we are committed to delivering cutting-edge software and hardware solutions infused with AI to meet the diverse needs of our clients and users.

Our Mission

To craft cutting-edge software empowered by Artificial Intelligence, aimed at delivering a sophisticated service while upholding an unwavering commitment to safeguarding user privacy. By seamlessly integrating advanced AI capabilities, our goal is to create a seamless and intuitive user experience that not only meets but exceeds expectations. Throughout the development process, we prioritize the implementation of robust privacy measures, ensuring that user data remains secure and confidential. With a dedication to ethical practices, our software seeks to redefine industry standards by striking a harmonious balance between technological innovation and the paramount importance of preserving user privacy.

Our Vision

To develop innovative software and hardware solutions, driven by the dual purpose of empowering businesses and positively impacting society. We aspire to create cutting-edge technology that not only provides businesses with powerful tools to extend their reach and enhance their operations but also focuses on services designed for widespread adoption, fostering positive change on a societal scale. Through our commitment to technological excellence and social responsibility, we aim to be a catalyst for progress, bridging the gap between businesses and the broader community, ensuring that our advancements contribute to a more inclusive, connected, and prosperous global landscape.
